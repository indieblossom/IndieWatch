Welcome!

Hey guys! It's Max here and this is basically a blog of my IndieWatch and progress on designs and software and functionality. If you have any questions, shoot me an email at indieblossom@gmail.com and I will get back to you.

ANOTHER SmartWatch? Really? Why?

There is no real good answer to this question. Best I can come up with is "because it is my own." I want my OWN watch that I can make MYSELF and design, MYSELF, because it is my watch, and I dished out the money for it. That is my problem with lots of things now-a-days. Everything is closed source and for profit, instead of making a cool invention and wanting to share it. That is another reason for this. I want to share my ideas and let people do what they please with them.

By all means, copy my designs, parts and code. Please. I won't bite ;) Open source is one of my favorite things in the world, well, besides cars!

Anyways, this is YOUR watch, along with MINE and I want YOUR input!

What can I do to make it better? What am I doing wrong? I want your input!!

Join the stand and OPENSOURCE it!
